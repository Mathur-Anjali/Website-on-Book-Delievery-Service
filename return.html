Returns

  1. If I receive a faulty/damaged packaged product from bookswagon, what should I do in that case?

    • Extensive quality checks are done during the packaging and shipping process for our customers. In any case, if anything improper is found with the delivered products by Bookswagon you can contact our customer service and they will be more than happy to assist you reach a resolution.

      Indian Customers:

      • If any product shipped to you is in faulty/damaged condition, you are asked by us to e-mail the images of the faulty/damaged packet, as soon as you open the packet. Once we receive the damaged packet, we will ship a duplicate order to you. In case the required product is out of stock, a full refund will be initiated.

  2. If I have to return the product ordered from bookswagon, how can I do that?

    • For Indian editions:

    Anyways if the product(s) purchased is no more required by you, you can write to us in that regard on “email-id” within 48 hours (2 days) of obtaining purchased order. You can also return the shipment, if unopened, for a repayment or gift voucher for the same amount. However, in such condition, we regret to inform that we would not be able to compensate for the shipping charges spend by you.
